All About Pixel Art
Gallery

Pixel art originated in early painting and drawing software, it became more popular in the 80's and the 90's by computer game design. Pixel art is created on graphics software that allows images to be edited at the individual pixel level. The images that are created are similar to early computer games or amateur-made mobile phone games, both of which were created by a pixel artist. Pixel art uses some of the basic techniques and principles of popular and ancient art such as pointillism and mosiac. Today, the continuation to see much more artistic and inventive methods and styles of using computer generated squares of color to make images. As computer design software constantly makes more and more progress, one can be certain that there will always be a spot reserved for pixel art.
